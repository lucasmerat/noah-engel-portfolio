Noah Engel is a Video Editor, Artist, and Filmmaker living in New York City, with over a decade of professional experience cutting Documentary, Commercial, and Branded Content.

Noah was born in New York, NY and received his BFA from New York University Tisch School of the Arts in Film & Television with a focus in Experimental Video and Performance Studies.

Clients include: The New Yorker, Bon Appétit, Vogue, Condé Nast Traveler, Adidas, SELF Magazine, Teen Vogue, GQ, Vanity Fair, Condé Nast Branded Entertainment, Senior Post, Likeable Media, Carbon Table, Area212, Intention Media, JS Casting, Spot Creative, Redbull Media House, ART DLR co, Ross Institute, and Longhouse Reserve, among others.

Noah's work has screened and been recognized by: Slamdance Film Festival, Florida Film Festival, Anthology Film Archives, Hong Kong Arthouse Film Festival, San Diego Underground Film Festival, Mirror Mountain Film Festival, Milchhof, Vimeo Staff Picks, DeepVimeo, and New York Film Week, among others.

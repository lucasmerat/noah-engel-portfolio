I am a Video Editor and Filmmaker based in NY, with a background in Documentary, Branded, and Narrative storytelling across formats.

Born and raised in NYC, I received a BFA from NYU Tisch School of the Arts in Film & Television, with a focus in Editing and a minor in Performance Studies. My professional work has encompassed a wide range of projects, from feature-length documentaries to advertising campaigns and branded social content. Working across both scripted and unscripted material has sharpened my skills in story developement and deepened my passion for crafting human centered narratives.

Clients have included major media brands, production companies, and cultural institutions such as The New Yorker, Vogue, Bon Appétit, GQ, Senior Post, and Vanity Fair, among others; Some select commercial clients include Adidas, McDonald's, Neutrogena, Ulta Beauty, Capital One; As well as creative studios and agencies including Condé Nast Entertainment, Likeable Media, and Spot Creative. I’ve also collaborated with independent production teams and nonprofits such as Carbon Table, Intention Media, and Ross Institute, among others.

As a filmmaker, I’ve had work recognized by Slamdance, Florida Film Festival, and Vimeo Staff Picks, with screenings at venues like Anthology Film Archives, Milchof Gallery, and beyond.

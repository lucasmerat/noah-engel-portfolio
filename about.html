I am a Video Editor and Filmmaker based in NY, with a background in Documentary, Branded, and Narrative storytelling across formats.

I was born and raised in New York. I received a BFA from NYU Tisch School of the Arts in Film & Television, with a focus in Editing and a minor in Performance Studies. My professional work has encompassed a wide range of projects, from feature-length documentaries to advertising campaigns and branded social content. Working across both scripted and unscripted material has sharpened my skills in story development and deepened my passion for crafting human centered narratives.

Clients have included major media brands such as The New Yorker, Vogue, Bon Appétit, GQ, and Vanity Fair.

Select recent commercial clients include Adidas, Clinique, L'Oreal, HSBC, and Stuart Weitzman.

I've worked with agencies, creative studios, and post houses including Condé Nast Entertainment, Likeable Media, Senior Post and Spot Creative.

As a filmmaker, I’ve had work recognized by Slamdance, Florida Film Festival, and Vimeo Staff Picks, with screenings at venues like Anthology Film Archives and beyond.
